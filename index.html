About SupraNS.name

SupraNS.name is a site with a list of SupraNS names that are available for purchase.
The list is maintained by the owner of the names and is updated regularly. The names are listed with their price, category, and other relevant information.

WTF is SupraNS?

It's the official namespace of the Supra network and entries registered in it are used to identify resources on the Supra network. You may access it here. Note that as of this moment you cannot browse entries, but you can search and view entries you do find.
SupraNS use cases are similar to Ethereum's ENS or Solana's Solana NS.
  • Primary function of SupraNS records is to identify Supra wallet addresses so that you can receive tokens or NFTs at toshua.supra instead of texting the sender and asking them to use 0xf0d0d7b6bdb66ca463269d0cd8e9abd6bc0c4d869f2a971769b3b81ca614d71c.
  • SupraNS names will also be used to identify other resources on the Supra network, on-chain reputation and more
The documentation with additional use cases can be found here.

How to buy

You can register new names and you can can pre-pay for one of these and have the ownership of one of these transferred to your wallet later.
As of now (February 2025) there's no way to transfer ownership of a SupraNS registration from a StarKey wallet to another. When that and a marketplace become available, these names will be listed at prices I intend to sell them for.
If you'd like to pay now and get the name when transfers becomes possible, reach out and escrow can be arranged. Here's how:
  • Agree on price (payable in $DEXUSDC)
  • Designate a mutually trusted community member from #price-discussion on Supra Discord to provide escrow service and release funds upon transfer of the name
  • When the the Supra or Dexlyn API or CLI or StarKey wallet can transfer SupraNS record ownership, the name will be transferred to the address from which you paid (unless indicated otherwise)
  • With an on-chain proof of transfer from SupraScan.io, your funds are claimed from the escrow person
You can swap Supra for dexUSDC here.
Do NOT send funds from a CEX wallet! Send them from StarKey address you control because that's the address your name will be transferred to.
Why pre-pay?
  • It's a way to reserve your name before a marketplace becomes available
  • It's a way to get a better price

Categories and notes:
  • AI - names that are suitable for AI
  • Gaming - names that are suitable for gaming
  • Meme - names that are suitable for memes
  • Business - names that are suitable for on-chain businesses (including freelancers)
  • Institutional - names that are suitable for institutions
  • Adult - names that are not suitable for children. (TRUE Adult means the name may not be explicit, but it's likely that it would be used for its implicit meaning)
  • Digits - names that are made of digits
  • Geo - names that are suitable for geo / location use
  • Fancy - general purpose names, basically "anything else"
  • Special - these will not be sold to random people (and, if you don't see any in the table at the bottom, that means they haven't even been listed)
