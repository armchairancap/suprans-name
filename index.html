Buy SupraNS names for StarKey and other Supra Network dApps

SupraNS.name is a site with a list of SupraNS names that are available for purchase. SupraNS names will be used in Supra Starkey wallet and other applications to identify resources on the Supra network.
The list is maintained by the owner of the names and is updated regularly. The names are listed with their price, category, and other relevant information.

WTF is the Supra Name Service (SNS) anyway?

It's the official namespace of the Supra network and entries registered in it are used to identify resources on the Supra network. You may access it here. Note that as of this moment you cannot browse entries, but you can search and view entries you do find.
SupraNS use cases are similar to Ethereum's ENS or Solana's Solana NS.
  • The primary function of SupraNS records is to identify Supra wallet addresses so that you can receive tokens or NFTs at (for example) toshua.supra instead of texting the sender and asking them to use 0xf0d0d7b6bdb66ca463269d0cd8e9abd6bc0c4d869f2a971769b3b81ca614d71c.
Find several other examples at the bottom of this page. The official SupraNS (SNS) documentation with additional use cases can be found here.

How to buy

You can register new names and you can can pre-pay for one of these and have the ownership of one of these transferred to your wallet later.
As of now (March 7, 2025) there's no way to transfer ownership of a SupraNS registration from a StarKey wallet to another. When that and a marketplace become available, these names will be listed at prices I intend to sell them for.
If you'd like to pay now and get the name when transfers becomes possible, reach out and escrow can be arranged. Here's how:
  • Agree on price (payable in $DEXUSDC)
  • Designate a mutually trusted community member from #price-discussion on Supra Discord to provide escrow service and release funds upon transfer of the name
  • When the the Supra or Dexlyn API or CLI or StarKey wallet can transfer SupraNS record ownership, the name will be transferred to the address from which you paid (unless indicated otherwise)
  • With an on-chain proof of transfer from SupraScan.io, your funds are claimed from the escrow person
You can swap Supra for dexUSDC here.
Do NOT send funds from a CEX wallet! Send them from a StarKey address you control because that's the address your SupraNS name will be transferred to.
Why pre-pay?
  • It's a way to reserve your name before a marketplace becomes available
  • It's a way to get a better price than you would in an auction

Use cases

Read the official Supra Name Service documentation to learn how to use it, but here are some examples:
  • Receive payments at yourname.supra instead of 0xf0d0d7b6bdb66ca463269d0cd8e9abd6bc0c4d869f2a971769b3b81ca614d71c. Share your wallet address verbally or in a chat app without looking it up.
  • Use game.yourname.supra as a username in a Supra dApp.
  • Use nft.yourname.supra for NFTs and tips.yourname.supra for tips (yourname.supra and tips.yourname.supra can optionally point to the same "default" address).
  • Use yourname.supra for one of your StarKey wallet addresses meant for personal use, and acme.supra for another from which you conduct freelance business. Both SNS names may be owned by one address. One advantage of using one wallet to manage is a single place to renew and manage SNS, as well as separation of concerns (e.g. a third address just for SNS management).
  • When SNS develops some of the features ENS and Solana NS have today, certain keys have non-chain values such as public IP addresses and then blog.yourname.supra could be set to resolve to your Web2 blog.
Currently (mid March 2025) you can pick one of your names to be the primary name (in case an address owns more than one), you may direct a name to another address, and you may create subdomains.
